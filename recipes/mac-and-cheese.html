Mac and Cheese

A creamy and cheesy pasta dish

A delightful and comforting meal made in less then 20 minutes using only 3 ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1lb Elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups Cheddar Cheese
  • 5 cups of milk

Steps

  1. Bring 5 cups of milk to a boil in a large pot
  2. Once boiling, add 1lb of your elbow macaroni in, and stir occansionally
  3. Once macaroni has reached desired doneness, take the pot off the heat and slowly fold in your cheddar
  4. Add salt, pepper, and any other desired seasoning, and enjoy
